How It Works

Our platform simplifies the process of creating and following personalized training plans

1. Create Account

Sign up as either a coach or athlete. Set up your profile with your specific needs and expertise.

2. Connect

Athletes can browse and connect with coaches. Coaches can manage their athlete roster.

3. Train & Track

Coaches create customized plans. Athletes follow and track progress with real-time feedback.

Who Is It For?

Our platform serves both coaches and athletes with specialized features

For Coaches

  • Create and manage multiple athlete profiles
  • Design personalized training plans with drag-and-drop interface
  • Track athlete progress with detailed analytics
  • Communicate directly through built-in messaging

For Athletes

  • Receive customized training plans from your coach
  • Track workouts and progress with easy-to-use interface
  • Get real-time feedback and adjustments
  • Access training resources and video demonstrations
